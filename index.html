FAQ

What kinds of things can I use LeagueRanker for?
LeagueRanker lets you get a good idea of the relative strengths of competitors in any ongoing struggle where there are wins and losses. You can enter office league results to aid in your cube-land smack talk. You can make a League and input results from your favorite professional sports teams to get an interesting perspective. Data goes in, analytics come out, otherwise do whatever floats your boat.
Why is your funding goal $0?
LeagueRanker is made by two guys working in tech jobs. We love competition, programming, and data, and this is a hobby project we're thrilled to be doing. We can cover the server deployment costs on our own, and since we're doing this for fun, we don't expect users to carry the team on this one.
What kinds of analytics do you do to pick the strongest teams?
Version 0 of the service which is in late stages of prototyping uses an elo ladder. Both members of the team have reasonably strong knowledge of machine learning technique, so if the project garners interest we'll likely get into saucier territory.
What do I get for contributing?
The warm fuzzy feeling of knowing our sweet sweet ec2 instances are running on your dime. We'll also listen to you for requests on analysis techniques. Otherwise diddly-squat.
